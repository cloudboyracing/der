Native Applications

The native mobile app variety is installed directly to the end users device via the Google Play store or Apple’s App store. A good example of a native app is your telephone app which you use daily to make calls from your mobile device. A native app is a true mobile application that uses the same programming languages to work on a singular platform. A native app made to work on just iOS will use Swift. A native app made to work on Android will use a language like Java.This means that end users will have to download these apps from the individual market places like the Apple store or the Google play store for each individual mobile device.

We develop any of our iOS and Android apps to the highest standard. We develop with your end users in mind. We take the guess work out of development. Our team does whatever it takes to complete your project on time and on budget. Contact us and see how we can help you go to the next level.

Progressive Web Applications 

Over the years I have been asked a lot of tech questions. The questions I get the most lately is what is a PWA?

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) are a perfect blend of multiple technologies to develop websites that perfectly resemble a mobile app. They bring together a perfect experience for the end user by giving them  the smooth “app-like” experience. It has the same UI experience that native apps are know for even though it is a website at the core. 

They are designed to be responsive and work well on all devices, ensuring the end user an awesome experience with improved performance and enhanced usability. This means that PWAs will someday have the ability to make a huge impact on your mobile web conversion rates. This will eliminate the need for native apps, App stores and will reduce your cost of deployment.
